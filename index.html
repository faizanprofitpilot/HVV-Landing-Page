Serving humanity through social entrepreneurship

Hunza Valley Ventures is a holding company dedicated to building and supporting businesses that create meaningful social impact. We believe in the power of entrepreneurship to address critical human needs while building sustainable, long-term value.

About

Social entrepreneurship sits at the intersection of business innovation and social good. At Hunza Valley Ventures, we focus on care-driven businesses that address fundamental human needs, from healthcare services to community support systems.

Our approach combines rigorous business discipline with deep commitment to improving lives, creating ventures that are both financially sustainable and socially transformative.

Leadership

Sultan Mehmood

Founder & CEO

Sultan Mehmood is the Founder & CEO of Hunza Valley Ventures. He brings decades of leadership experience across humanitarian response, program management, and social impact initiatives, including senior roles with the United Nations World Food Programme.

His work has focused on serving vulnerable communities through sustainable, mission-driven organizations. At Hunza Valley Ventures, he leads with a commitment to humanity, integrity, and long-term impact.
